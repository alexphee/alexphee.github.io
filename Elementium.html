What is Elementium?

Elementium is a retro style, rpg-inspired game developed to help students grasp the basics of chemistry. After the game is finished the student is expected to have a better understanding of element and compound names, compound forming and their connection to everyday life. The contents of the game are loosely based on Greek junior high school Chemistry books.
